About Me

I am currently a Junior Researcher at Waseda University (JSPS-funded postdoctoral research fellow) since Oct. 2023. I received my Ph.D. in Sep. 2022 from Kyoto University under the supervision of Jun Kigami.

I am currently working on Non-linear potential theory on fractals and Quasiconformal geometry of the underlying spaces with particular emphasis on Analysis & Geometry of the Sierpinski carpets. For details, please see my RESEARCH page. I am also interested in Analysis/Probability on metric measure spaces including random media.
