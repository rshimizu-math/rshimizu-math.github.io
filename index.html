About Me

I am currently an Assistant Professor at Kyoto University. I completed my Ph.D. in September 2022 at Kyoto University and my Ph.D. supervisor was Jun Kigami. From April 2023 to March 2025, I was a JSPS-funded Junior Researcher at Waseda University under the supervision of Takashi Kumagai. I then came back to Kyoto University in April 2025.

My research interest lies in the interplay between nonlinear potential theory on fractals and quasiconformal geometry of the underlying spaces. To be specific, I study \(p\)-energy forms, \(p\)-energy measures, \(p\)-harmonic functions and Ahlfors regular conformal dimension on self-similar fractals. For details, please visit RESEARCH. My research interest also includes Analysis/Geometry on metric measure spaces, on (random) disordered media and on (hyperbolic) groups.
