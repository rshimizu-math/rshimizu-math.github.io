About Me

I am currently an Assistant Professor in the Applied Analysis Group at Graduate School of Informatics, Kyoto University. I completed my Ph.D. in September 2022 at Kyoto University under the supervision of Jun Kigami. I was a JSPS-funded Junior Researcher at Waseda University, working with Takashi Kumagai, from April 2023 to March 2025, and then came back to Kyoto University in April 2025.

My research interest lies in the interplay between nonlinear potential theory on fractals and quasiconformal geometry of the underlying spaces. In particular, I am currently working on \(p\)-energy forms, \(p\)-energy measures, \(p\)-harmonic functions and Ahlfors regular conformal dimension on self-similar fractals. For details, please visit RESEARCH. My research interest also includes Analysis/Geometry on metric measure spaces, on (random) disordered media and on (hyperbolic) groups.
