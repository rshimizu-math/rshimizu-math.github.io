About Me

I am currently a Junior Researcher at Waseda University (JSPS-funded postdoctoral research fellow) since Oct. 2023. I received my Ph.D. in Sep. 2022 from Kyoto University under the supervision of Jun Kigami.

My research interest lies in the interplay between nonlinear potential theory on fractals and quasiconformal geometry of the underlying spaces. To be specific, on self-similar fractals, I study \(p\)-energy forms, \(p\)-energy measures, \(p\)-harmonic functions and Ahlfors regular conformal dimension. For details, please visit RESEARCH.  My research interest also includes Analysis/Geometry on metric measure spaces, on (random) disordered media and on (hyperbolic) groups.
